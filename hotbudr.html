budr_story

Shatz and Megan met in linear algebra at the University of Florida.
Shatz designed the the hot_budr logo in 2018 and began work on the app later that year.
Megan saw the potential for the brand to grow and started an Instagram page at the beginning of 2019.
In June 2019, the hot_budr app was officially released for both IOS and android.
Shatz continues to maintain and promote the app, while Megan directs social media endeavors.
They hope that everyone enjoys the absurdity of hot_budr!
